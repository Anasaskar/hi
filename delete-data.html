‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use CloyAI again.

Will I receive a refund for my paid subscription?

Refunds are handled according to our Terms of Service. Generally, we do not provide refunds for partial subscription periods. Please contact support@cloyai.com to discuss your specific situation.

How long does the deletion process take?

Your account is deactivated immediately. Complete deletion from all systems, including backups, typically takes up to 30 days.

What if I signed in with Google or Facebook?

Deleting your CloyAI account does not affect your Google or Facebook account. However, you should also revoke CloyAI's access in your Google/Facebook account settings:

  • Google: Visit Google Account Permissions
  • Facebook: Visit Facebook Apps and Websites

Can I delete specific data instead of my entire account?

You can delete individual images from your account by logging in and managing your uploads. For other data deletion requests, contact support@cloyai.com.

What are my other privacy rights?

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you also have rights to access, correct, export, and restrict processing of your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
